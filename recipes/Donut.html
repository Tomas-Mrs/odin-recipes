Donuts

Fluffy, fragrant and filled with great jam. These are delicious donuts.
Give the dough enough time to rise and prepare carefully for frying.
You will be rewarded with perfect loaves with the typical white stripe.

Ingredients:

  • 530 g all-purpose flour
  • 180 g coarse flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 80 g granulated sugar
  • 20 g rum
  • 120 g softened butter
  • 300 g milk
  • 40 g yeast
  • 6 g salt
  • 1 liter frying oil
  • 50 g powdered sugar for sprinkling
  • 400 g jam for filling

Procedure:

  1. Mix milk and coarse flour with yeast and let it rise covered for 30 minutes.
  2. After rising, add the remaining ingredients and knead into a non-sticky dough.
    Allow it to rise slightly for another 30 minutes, then divide it into 50g portions and shape them well.
  3. On a floured surface, let them rise covered with a clean cloth until they triple in size.
  4. In a pot, heat rapeseed oil to a height of about 4 cm to a temperature of 170Â°C,
    and carefully place the risen pastries into it with the risen side facing down.
  5. Fry on each side for about 2 minutes until golden brown.
    Carefully remove them onto a tray lined with paper towels and let them drain and cool.
  6. After cooling, make a hole in the side with a sharp knife and
    fill the pastries with jam or prune jam using a pastry bag.
  7. Sprinkle the filled pastries with sugar and serve.
